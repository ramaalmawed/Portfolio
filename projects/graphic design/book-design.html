Alzenins Books & Print Materials

A collection of educational books, calendars, planners, and notebook designs created for Alzenins, an online Japanese learning platform for Arabic-speaking students.

Project Context

Alzenins is an online Japanese learning platform where I work as a co-founder, Japanese language teacher, graphic designer, and also support administrative work together with the other co-founders.

My Role

I created the learning content, Arabic explanations, Japanese examples, structure, layout, page design, and print preparation. I also planned how the materials should support our students during lessons.

AI Visual Support

AI tools were used to generate some of the visual illustration elements. My work focused on selecting, adapting, arranging, and integrating these visuals into a consistent educational design system.

Language Focus

The materials are created for Arabic-speaking students learning Japanese. Because of this, the content is written in Japanese and Arabic only, without English or Swedish.

Confidentiality

I cannot show the full grammar book because it is currently used as Alzenins teaching material and will later be prepared for sale.

Japanese Grammar Book

The first and most important project in this collection is a Japanese grammar book for beginner-level students. The book teaches Japanese through Arabic explanations, Japanese examples, exercises, vocabulary lists, and guided lesson structure.

Grammar Book — Level 1

This book was designed as structured teaching material for Alzenins students. The goal was to make Japanese grammar easier, more visual, and more accessible for Arabic-speaking learners.

I created the educational content myself because I teach Japanese and have passed JLPT N3. The book includes beginner grammar, numbers, pronouns, self-introduction, example sentences, exercises, and vocabulary.

I designed the page structure, layout, typography, exercise pages, content flow, and visual hierarchy. AI tools were used for some visual illustration elements, while I handled the direction, selection, layout integration, and final composition.

The full book cannot be shown publicly because it is part of Alzenins teaching material. We currently use it during lessons, and in the future we plan to sell it as part of our learning products.

Tools: Adobe InDesign · Adobe Illustrator · Adobe Photoshop · PowerPoint · AI Visual Tools

Calendar Design

A Japanese-themed calendar designed for Alzenins, combining soft visual design, Japanese vocabulary, kanji, hiragana readings, Arabic meanings, and seasonal themes.

Japanese Learning Calendar

This calendar was created as both a visual product and a small learning experience. Each month introduces a Japanese word or kanji with Arabic meaning and a calm seasonal design.

My role included planning the layout, choosing the monthly structure, arranging Japanese and Arabic text, preparing the calendar pages, and making the design feel consistent with the Alzenins learning atmosphere.

AI tools were used for the visual illustration part. I did not draw the illustration elements myself; instead, I worked on the design direction, layout, typography, composition, and print-ready structure.

Tools: Adobe InDesign · Adobe Illustrator · Adobe Photoshop · AI Visual Tools

Notebooks & Planners

A collection of notebooks, sketchbooks, and weekly planners created for Alzenins. These products support writing, studying, planning, and creative learning.

Notebook, Sketchbook & Weekly Planner

These materials were designed to match the visual identity and learning feeling of Alzenins. They include notebook covers, writing pages, weekly planner layouts, notes sections, and study-friendly page structures.

I worked on the layout, page structure, print preparation, typography, and overall composition. AI tools were used for some decorative visual elements, while I handled how those visuals were used inside the final designs.

These products are part of a larger direction for Alzenins. We plan to create more books, notebooks, planners, and study products in the future.

Tools: Adobe InDesign · Adobe Illustrator · Adobe Photoshop · AI Visual Tools
